1. Welcome to TPC

Welcome to the custom-built TPC Employee Portal. This platform is designed to make your job as easy as possible by showing you exactly what you need to focus on, without the clutter of other departments.

Your Workflow: You do not need to assign tasks to yourself or pass them to others. When it is your turn to work on a project, it will automatically appear in your dashboard. When you finish it, click a button, and it automatically moves to the next person!

2. Your Dashboard

When you log in at /employee, you will see a table containing your active projects.

  • Targeted Tasks: The table only contains tasks that have been explicitly assigned to you based on your role.
  • Priority Sorting: Tasks are automatically sorted so that the most urgent ones (closest due date) appear at the top.
  • Accessing Work: Look at the Links column. Click on the link icons to instantly open the resources required for your specific stage (e.g., Google Docs for scripts, or Google Drive folders for raw footage).

Submitting Your Work

Once you have finished the work required for a task (e.g., you finished writing the script, or you finished editing the video):

  • 1. Locate the task in your dashboard.
  • 2. Click the green Mark as Done button in the Action column.
  • 3. The task is instantly sent to your Department Lead Admin for review!

Fixes Required: If your Admin rejects the work, the task will return to your dashboard with a red Fixes Required status. You will also see a red note explaining exactly what needs to be fixed. Fix it, and click Mark as Done again.

3. Role-Specific Guides

For Content Writers

Your primary stage is Scripting.

  • Open the attached Google Doc link from your dashboard.
  • Write the reel/video script or ad copy.
  • Click Mark as Done to send it to Garv for review. If approved, it moves to the videographers automatically.

For Videographers

Your primary stage is Shooting.

  • Review the approved script for the project.
  • Shoot the video.
  • Upload the raw, unedited footage to the provided Google Drive link (Drive Link A).
  • Click Mark as Done to send it to Garv for review. If approved, it is handed off to the Editors automatically.

For Editors

Your primary stage is Editing.

  • Download the raw footage from your dashboard (Drive Link B).
  • Edit the video to TPC's high standards (fast pacing, strong hooks).
  • Upload the final exported video to the Final Output link (Drive Link C).
  • Click Mark as Done to send it to Dhruv for final quality control.

4. Website CMS Access

Note: This section only applies to users with the CONTENT WRITER role.

If your role involves writing blogs for the main website, you will see a prominent Website CMS button at the top of your personal dashboard.

  • Clicking this takes you to the Content Management System.
  • Here, you can create, edit, and publish rich-text blog posts directly to the live website.
  • Use the familiar, Google Docs-style editor to format your posts without needing to write code. What you see in the editor is exactly how it will look on the live website.
